Architectural Type

Form Meets Force

Monument Extended (Primary): Unapologetic. Structural. Weight. This typeface is a physical force that occupies space, mirroring the stability of the athlete.

PP Neue Montreal (Secondary): Clinical Luxury. Precision. Strategy. The sophisticated counter-balance providing editorial clarity and refinement.

Color System

Physiological Anchors

Performance Red (#B71D23): The kinetic trigger. High-energy contrast used strictly for data points and calls-to-action.
Oxblood (#481E1F): The shadow of effort. Adds depth and a "blood, sweat, and iron" grit to the visual narrative.
Carbon Black (#000000): The foundation of dominance. It represents total immersion and the removal of distraction.
Champagne Bronze (#D0BFA6): The elite standard. Replaces neutral gray to signify industrial luxury and achievement.
